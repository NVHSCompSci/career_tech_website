Education and training is a two year, three period per year, career tech class taught by former preschool intervention specialist Mr. Eickholt. Through the class students can get a feel for teaching as well as practical skills they can take with them into college and their careers.

“It’s a behind the scenes look at what teaching is all about”
-Mr. Eickholt
Through the programs students learn how to lesson plan, teach a variety of different ages, and gain skills they can bring with them into college and their careers.

Junior year

Junior year students work on lesson planning and then apply what they learned by teaching in the Northview Preschool.

Senior year

Senior year students get to shadow elementary school teachers at one of the districts elementary schools.

Benefits

College Credit

Students can earn up to 5 credit hours during their senior year.

Certifications

Students can earn certifications in CPR, first aid, child abuse awareness, communicable diseases and ParaPro Assessment
