About our Teachers

After graduating from Northview High School, Mr. Sayre decided to return and continue his educational journey right where it all began. He initially intended to pursue a career as a social studies teacher after graduation, but he took a different path. Coaching college basketball. His dedication and passion for education, however, led him to pursue further academic achievements, obtaining an MBA with the financial support of Northview. Alongside Mr. Sayre, Mrs. Martin currently teaches at Northview. Mrs. Martin, equipped with a master's in education and a background in teaching business, complements Mr. Sayre's expertise. Both educators are qualified to teach College Credit Plus (CCP) classes, utilizing Mrs. Martin's master's in education and Mr. Sayre's master's in business. Together, they contribute to the academic enrichment of Northview, bringing a wealth of knowledge and experience to the students they instruct.


Benifits of the Program

Immerse yourself in the myriad benefits our program offers. From diverse learning paths to hands-on projects, we tailor your education. Gain invaluable college credits, project-based skills, and a compass for future careers. BPA competitions, and a two-period structure to enrich your education. Inclusivity defines us; whether you're a business novice or enthusiast, our program ensures you thrive.


College Credit Opportunities

Our dynamic Business Tech program is tailored for all learning levels, the curriculum spans regular, honors, and College Credit Plus (CCP) classes. Students can earn 12-15 college credit hours, covering essential entry-level subjects applicable to any college. No prerequisites are necessary, ensuring an inclusive start for all students. The program's project-based approach ensures practical, real-world application of knowledge. Seniors gain invaluable field experience through internships, setting the stage for seamless transitions into the professional world. Business Professionals of America (BPA) competitions provide a competitive edge, showcasing skills at regional, state, and national levels. Our program serves as a compass, guiding students toward diverse career paths in sales, accounting, and more.
