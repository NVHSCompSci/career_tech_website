iMedia

In this course, we delve into the essential pillars of modern communication and technology. We're all living in an era driven by digital innovation, and possessing skills in Information Technology, Design Techniques, Digital Graphics, Video and Sound editing, and Web Design is more crucial than ever. Join us as we unlock the creative potential within Adobe Creative Cloud, empowering you to craft engaging visuals and seamless web designs. From mastering digital graphics to harnessing the power of sound and video, we'll guide you through hands-on projects and real-world applications. Regardless of your career path, these skills are fundamental in our technologically-driven world. Equip yourself with the tools and knowledge needed to stand out and excel in this dynamic digital landscape. Are you ready to create, innovate, and leave a lasting digital footprint? Let's shape the future together.


What To Expect

In your Interactive Media class, you'll delve into a wide array of multimedia skills. From foundational design to photo editing and production. You'll also master audio manipulation and learn logo and graphic design to create engaging digital content. This class equips you to blend creativity and technology, crafting impactful interactive media.

Benifits of the Program

The Interactive Media program offers a valuable jumpstart for those aspiring to pursue a career in media. Providing exposure to industry-standard software utilized in the workforce, the program ensures that students acquire practical skills essential for success in the field. The opportunity to earn college credits through the Career Technical Credit Transfer program in areas such as Electronic Publishing, Digital Video Production, Raster Graphics, and Internet & Web Languages is a significant advantage, offering a head start in higher education. Importantly, the program isn't solely focused on preparing for a media career; it guarantees a lot of fun and creativity, even for those who may choose a different path.

College Credit Opportunities

One standout feature of the Interactive Media program is the opportunity to earn college credits through the Career Technical Credit Transfer program. By successfully completing courses in Electronic Publishing, Digital Video Production, Raster Graphics, and Internet & Web Languages, students not only gain practical, hands-on skills but also accrue college credit. This not only accelerates their progress in higher education but also provides a tangible advantage as they embark on their academic and professional journeys. The ability to earn college credits while still in high school is a significant benefit, offering students a head start in their pursuit of a media-related education and career.
