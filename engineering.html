Engineering Tech

About

The engineering Technology program offers many different oppurtunities for Sylvania Highschool students looking to do engineering in the future or those who just want to learn more about engineering.

"[The] Engineering program is extremely fun, and teaches skills used for many years in your life, and for many years to come."
-Mr. Reed
In addition to four career tech courses (aerospace, manufacturing operations, robotics and electrical systems) the engineering program offers an abundance of intro courses for those looking to jumpstart their engineering education!
