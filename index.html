Northview Career Technical Program


Sylvania Northview High School offers a large variety of career technical education (CTE) programs to prepare students to join the workforce or continue with their career-specific education in college. From Computer Science to Medical Technologies, students can take these career technical classes starting their junior year, where they will be committed to a two-year program. Through these CTE programs, they will learn the respective ropes of their chosen career.


Opportunities

Students that choose to partake in these CTE programs will be given the chance to earn workforce certification and market themselves to potential employers using LinkedIn. For example, Medical Technology students will be able to earn a CPR certification and a STNA certificate. In many programs, students will partake in rigorous classes where communication is stressed through large group projects. Honors-level, AP classes, and college credits are available in the vast majority of CTE programs.



Programs offered

Northview offers eight career technical programs. The respective programs are:

  • Buisness Technology
  • Computer Science
  • Cosmetology
  • Education Training
  • Engineering
  • Family & Consumer Science
  • Interactive Media
  • Medical Technologies


Applying

The CTE programs are available to join for all juniors at Northview High School. The application process starts in the second semester of a student's sophmore year. Career technical days are offered to sophmores, in which they can explore potential CTE programs. Applications are not judged on a first-come, first-serve basis. Rather, they are judged on a student's academic prowess, high school attendance history, and previous disipline records.
