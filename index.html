Career Technical Program

Sylvania Northview High School offers a large variety of career technical classes to help prepare students to join the workforce. From Computer Science to Medical Technologies, Northview can prepare students to join the workforce immediately after graduation or pursue a higher education in college. Students can take these classes starting their junior year, where they will be in a two-year program in which they will learn the respective ropes of their chosen career.

Opportunities

Students that choose to partake in these career technical classes will be given the chance to earn workforce certification and market themselves to potential employers using LinkedIn. They will partake in rigorous classes where communication is stressed through large group projects. Through these classes, they will learn to use the tools of their respective trade.




Programs offered

Northview offers eight career technical programs. The respective programs are:

  • Buisness Technology
  • Computer Science
  • Cosmetology
  • Family & Consumer Science
  • Education Training
  • Engineering
  • Interactive Media
  • Medical Technologies
