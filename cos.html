Cosmetology


Overview of Course

Discover the artistry of Cosmetology at Northview with our engaging course structure tailored for students of various levels. Juniors will work on their Cosmetology skills during periods 1-5, while seniors will refine their skills during periods 5-9. Although not aspiring cosmetologists can lay the groundwork for their journey by taking Cosmetology Foundations as a freshman or Sophomore to determine the suitability.

What do you do in Cosmetology

This comprehensive course covers an array of disciplines. Dive into the art of hair with hands-on training in styling, cutting, coloring, highlights, and relaxers. Become an expert at doing nails with manicures, pedicures, nail art, and artificial nails. Dive into the realm of skincare through waxing, facials, and makeup application. This dynamic curriculum ensures that you're equipped with skills needed to thrive in the world of beauty.

About College Credits

This program allows you to obtain college credits through an articulation agreement with Owens Tech. Upon successful completion of the course and obtaining board certification from the Ohio State Board of Cosmetology. you can earn 15 credit hours towards Owens' Business Program. This unique opportunity will help you get a head start on your college Education!



Other Benefits and Opportunities

One notable advantage is the ability to obtain your license before graduating high school, providing you with a head start in the industry. This license is applicable to various fields in the industry such as hairdressing, nail technology, and more. Seniors have the chance to gain real-world experience through field placement opportunities interning in salons. Test your skills against other people by participating in competitions such as Skills USA, where you can showcase your talent at the local, regional, and state level.
