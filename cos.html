Overview of Course

Discover the artistry of Cosmetology at Northview with our engaging course structure tallored for students of various levels. Juniors will work on their Cosmetology skills during periods 1-5, while seniors will refine their skills during periods 5-9. Although not aspiring cosmetologists can lay the groundwork for their journey by taking Cosmetology Foundations as a freshman or Sophmore to determine the suitability.

What do you do in Cosmetology

This comprehensive course covers an array of disciplines. Dive into the art of hair with hands-on training in styling, cutting, coloring, highlights, and relaxers. Become an expert at doing nails with manicures, pedicures, nail art, and artificial nails. Dive into the realm of skincare through waxing, facials, and makeup application. This dynamic cirriculum ensures that your equiped with skills needed to thrive in the world of beauty.

About College Credits

The program has an articulation agreement with Owens Tech If you complete the course and get board certified (Ohio State Board of Cosmetology), you are eligible to get 15 credit hours at Owens toward their Business Program.
